Eligibility for Air Force X Group (Agniveer Vayu) 2026-27

  • Age: 17.5 to 21 years (typically born between Jan 2005 – Jul 2008; confirm in latest notification)
  • Education: 12th with Physics, Mathematics & English (minimum 50% aggregate + 50% in English)
    OR 3-year Diploma in Engineering (50% marks) OR Vocational Course with Physics & Math
  • Gender: Unmarried Male / Female candidates
  • Physical Standards: Height min. 152.5 cm, good medical fitness

Note: Always check the official notification on agnipathvayu.cdac.in or careerairforce.gov.in for exact dates and details.

Selection Process (Brief)

1. Online Written Exam (English, Physics, Maths + Reasoning/GA for some)
2. Physical Fitness Test (1.6 km run in \~6:30 min, Push-ups, Sit-ups, etc.)
3. Adaptability Tests
4. Medical Examination

We provide complete guidance on syllabus, previous papers, physical preparation, and common mistakes to avoid.

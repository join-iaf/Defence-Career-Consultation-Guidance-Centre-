Defence Career Consultation & Guidance Centre

About Us

At Defence Career Consultation & Guidance Centre, our mission is to provide the right direction to rural youth who aspire to join the Indian Air Force, specifically in the X Group. Many students complete their 12th with good marks but lack proper guidance, which leads to confusion and wasted time.

Why Join Us

Maine dekha hai ki gaon ke bahut saare bachche 12th ke baad apna career decide nahi kar paate. Proper counselling na hone ki wajah se wo bhatak jaate hain. Isi problem ko solve karne ke liye ye centre banaya gaya hai jahan sahi guidance milegi.

About Me

The guidance provided here is based on real experience and practical knowledge. The aim is to help students avoid mistakes and achieve success with proper strategy, discipline, and dedication.
